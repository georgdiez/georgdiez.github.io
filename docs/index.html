Georg Diez, PhD

I completed my PhD in theoretical Physics at the University of Freiburg in the group of Prof. Gerhard Stock, where I worked at the interface of physics and biology, combining machine learning with physics-informed modeling to understand protein dynamics.

My expertise lies in translating complex problems into actionable insights through data-driven analysis. I develop predictive models that uncover hidden patterns in high-dimensional systems—enabling data-driven strategies and more informed decision-making.
