Data Scientist & Computational Physicist

I build predictive models and develop data-driven methods to understand complex, high-dimensional systems. I hold a PhD in theoretical Physics from the University of Freiburg (group of Prof. Gerhard Stock), where I worked at the interface of physics and machine learning.

My expertise lies in turning complex, high-dimensional data into actionable insights—uncovering hidden patterns and enabling more informed decision-making.
